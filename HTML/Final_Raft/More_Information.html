Golden Path


Join us at White Water Rafting and experience one of our three getaways. Theres the golden path which features some of yellowstones rapids. Its called the Golden Path due to the yellow rocks in the river stream. The leaves nearby in the fall glitter the river with their red and yellow leaves making this the perfect family photo at the end of your trip.

Rabbit Rapids

Next is the Rabbit Rapids this is mainly for our newcommers who have never had a chance to quickly get used to this new experience. You will have the opportunity to go over our mild rapids course. You will also be able to enjoy all the twists and turns and small drops.

Ragged Rapids

Finally there is Ragged Rapids these rapids are for our more experienced rafters. These rapids will have steep drops and can cause injuryies if not careful.

We hope to see you out on the Rapids with us!
